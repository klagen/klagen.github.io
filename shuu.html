Case

Use the logo and create a website that sells shoes.

The process

During a four hour long workshop the UI for Shuu was created. The process began with the creation of a moodboard to ensure that we would end up with a consistent style on the page. A greybox (low-fidelity design with focus on functionality) was created before the pictures, fonts and colors were added to the final design.

Our aim

Let's create a website for a sneakers lover with a passion for sustainable fashion.

The solution

This website is directed towards the urban youth that has a passion for sustainable fashion - the sneakers are made from recycled or sustainable materials, and worn-out shoes can be sent back to the company to avoid landfill.

The overall design is simple to ensure that the page visitor can orient themselves easily and quickly find the sneakers of their dreams.
