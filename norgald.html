This project was performed on demand of norgald - a cyber security company that offers a powergrid sequrity system to it's clients. norgald requested a new design for their website (desktop and mobile interface design), as well as an overall graphic profile for the company.

Our team produced an elegant design for the website, with a black and purple theme to make the company stand out in the cyber sequrity-crowd, where blue or sometimes green color themes are dominant.

The design is responsive and fulfills the WCAG-requirements. A darker layout with vector graphics instead of an image heavy website was chosen, to minimize the website's impact on the environment.

A stylesheet was developed, complete with a unique and contrastful color scheme, a highly readable font and logotypes for norgald as well as their software system Elvira.
