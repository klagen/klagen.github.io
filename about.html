Who is Klara?

Klara holds a BSc in design engineering and has a passion for UX- and UI design. She likes to spend her spare time creating interactive UI prototypes and graphic designs. Klara is always eager to improve her skills, learn new softwares and find new ways to create great design solutions.

She is a great team player with a positive mindset. At work she is thorough and result oriented, however she is not afraid to replace an idea with an even better one if the added effort will benefit the outcome.

Work experience

ESSIQ AB, Gothenburg (2018-2021)

At ESSIQ I worked as a Design Engineer. I performed an assignment for Volvo Car Corporation, where I was a part of the thermal management team. While working as a Group Design Leader I made sure that information about the current design status was available to everyone who was involved - design engineers as well as suppliers. By creating complex documents that were still easy to orient oneself in, the communication was improved and the product development was made easier to verify.

The lively process and high pace that the product development entailed taught me the importance of thorough organization as well as quick and clear communication.

Atea AB, Luleå/Remote (2018)

I was asked by Atea AB to help them in a UI assignment that was requested by one of their major customers. With short notice, I developed wireframes that were used as a blueprint by the programmers who developed an application for the customer.

Espresso House, Gothenburg (May 2016 - November 2018)

At Espresso House I worked as a shift leader. My work assignments consisted of delivering great customer service while preventing and averting complications during the work shift. This assignment taught me the value of effective communication and the act of planning ahead, in order to avoid unexpected issues. As a result I transformed into a positive team player with the ability to organize and streamline my work assignments.

Röselidsskolan, Gråbo (2014 - 2015)

At Röselidsskolan I worked in the primary school department (5-8 year olds). I was responsible for a group of six year olds that consisted of 17 children. My work assignments consisted of planning and teaching Swedish and mathematics. My greatest learning during this employment is the insight into differences amongst people, and the importance of answering to their individual needs in the best ways possible.
